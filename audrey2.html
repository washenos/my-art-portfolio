Art Process

Step 1: Sketch

So first I draw the sketch first (basically like an idea on what you're gonna draw.) I do use references from pinterest sometimes.

Step 2: Base Colouring

So I colour out the base. I choose my colours by what I just think looks right, I dont really follow a "colour theory" which is probably bad.

Step 3: Colouring Details

Lastly, I put some colouring details such as shading, gradients, highlights, etc. Then I finish.
